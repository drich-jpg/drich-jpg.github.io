Noise Pollution in San Diego

San Diego County is a busy hub of all sorts of activity, from its downtown urban developments, to its military bases, and to its busy freeway systems. As someone who prefers peace and quiet, I developed this map to depict souces of distracting noise. Studies in noise profiles may be especially useful for neighborhood planning and school developments, since students especially need to be able to focus on their studies. This project was completed utilizing ESRI ArcMap for a GIS class final project in Spring 2018.
